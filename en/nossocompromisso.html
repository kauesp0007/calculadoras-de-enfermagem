Our Commitment to Digital Sustainability

We believe in a more conscious, accessible, and eco-friendly digital world.

Low-Impact Hosting

Currently, our hosting is not certified by The Green Web Foundation or other renewable energy initiatives. We recognize this limitation and therefore compensate with concrete measures in other areas of digital sustainability, such as code optimization, data consumption reduction, and accessibility best practices. We continue to evaluate providers with lower environmental impact and will consider future migration whenever technically feasible and financially sustainable.

Efficient Code

We use optimized Tailwind CSS with unused classes removed, images are compressed in WebP format and use loading="lazy", and we avoid unnecessary heavy libraries.

Accessibility as a Principle

We apply accessibility best practices to make our content inclusive: color contrast according to WCAG AAA standard, navigation compatible with screen readers and keyboard, use of semantic landmarks.

Impact Monitoring

We monitor our digital footprint using tools such as: Website Carbon Calculator and EcoGrader.

Impact Reports

We publish periodic reports on: estimated energy consumption per page, improvements implemented to reduce weight and requests, and accessibility and usability evaluated by independent tools.
