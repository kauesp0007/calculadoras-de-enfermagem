Apgar Score

Assesses the physical condition of a newborn immediately after birth.

The Apgar score assesses the physical condition of a newborn at two crucial moments: 1 and 5 minutes of life. It evaluates five criteria: heart rate, respiratory effort, muscle tone, reflex irritability, and skin color.
