Ballard Score

Ballard Score – Newborn Gestational Age Assessment

The Ballard Score is a validated tool to estimate newborn gestational age based on physical and neuromuscular signs. This calculator helps healthcare professionals evaluate both premature and term infants quickly and reliably.
