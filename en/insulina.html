Syringe measurements for subcutaneous medication administration - 1 mL Insulin syringe:

Examples:

100 IU equals 1 mL or 1 cc

50 IU equals 0.5 mL or 0.5 cc

30 IU equals 0.3 mL or 0.3 cc

(Prefer insulin syringes with a capacity of 100 IU)

Attention: 1 IU of Insulin equals 0.01 mL > 10 IU equals 0.1 mL

ATTENTION: INSULIN IS A HIGH ALERT MEDICATION (HAM), PERFORM DOUBLE CHECK WITH YOUR NURSE.
