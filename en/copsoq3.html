Mathematical Evaluation Logic

Likert Scale: Answers are quantified from 1 to 5.

Raw Global Average: Unlike isolated interpretations of subscales recommended for in-depth corporate consulting, this web screening tool extracts a simple average from all answered questions in the matrix blocks to provide a general alert overview (Where higher values indicate a stronger presence of the sum of demands, stressors, or reported dissatisfaction).

Thresholds (Terciles): ≤ 2.33 (Green/Favorable), 2.34 to 3.65 (Yellow/Attention), and ≥ 3.66 (Red/Psychosocial Risk).
