Johns Hopkins Fall Risk Scale

Patient Safety Goal 6: Reduce the risk of falls and injuries resulting from falls in healthcare settings.

This scale aims to identify hospitalized patients at risk of falling, classifying them as low, moderate, or high risk based on clinical, cognitive, and functional factors. Its application allows for effective and individualized preventive measures.
