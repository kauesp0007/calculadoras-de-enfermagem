Digital Impact Report

Base year: 2025

Objective

This report aims to present the first actions focused on reducing the environmental impact of our website, promoting transparency and reinforcing our commitment to sustainability in the digital health sector. Although the site was launched recently (June 2025), we have already implemented relevant measures and are in the process of continuously measuring our environmental performance.

Sustainable Practices Adopted (ongoing)

Indicator Current Status
Hosting focused on energy efficiency Yes
Use of WebP image format 100%
Reduction in use of external libraries Ongoing
Modular structure and reusable code Progressive implementation
Average page weight Under measurement
Performance and CO‚ÇÇ monitoring tools Website Carbon, EcoGrader, Lighthouse
Ethical and lightweight cookie script Yes

Partial Results (Initial Estimates)

Despite the recent creation of the site, we have already observed promising preliminary results:

  • Resource consumption reduction: Lightweight pages with modular structure contribute to lower data and energy usage.
  • Continuous performance improvement: We periodically monitor and adjust page loading, reducing unnecessary scripts and prioritizing critical resources.
  • Accessibility and efficiency: Faster navigation also means less screen time, lower energy consumption, and a better user experience.

Tools Used for Measurement and Monitoring

  • Website Carbon Calculator
  • EcoGrader
  • Google Lighthouse
  • Chrome DevTools
  • GTmetrix, Pingdom

Commitment to Digital Sustainability

We believe every website can contribute to a more conscious digital environment. Even without all consolidated data, we have followed sustainable development principles since the beginning of the project. Our goal is to achieve reference standards such as:

  • Emission below 0.2g of CO‚ÇÇ per visit
  • Average reduction of 50% in page weight by the end of 2025
  • Hosting with Green Web Foundation seal by 2026

Next Steps

  • Publication of the first full report with auditable metrics in 2026
  • Adoption of annual digital efficiency goals
  • Integration of automated environmental reports into the site's maintenance routine
