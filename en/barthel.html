Barthel Scale

Assessment of Functional Dependence in Daily Activities

The Barthel Scale is a tool used to assess functional dependence in daily activities, especially in people with physical or neurological disabilities. It measures the individual's ability to perform 10 basic tasks, assigning scores that indicate the degree of dependence.
