CRIES Scale - Pain Assessment in Newborns

A tool for assessing pain in newborns, especially useful in postoperative situations.

The CRIES scale is a tool to assess pain in newborns, especially useful in postoperative situations. It analyzes five behavioral and physiological parameters that may indicate pain.
