Purpose of the Website

To provide nursing professionals and students with a practical tool for calculating medications and doses based on medical prescriptions, simulations, or course assignments. The user enters the requested data, and the calculator automatically performs the necessary mathematical calculations.

We also provide applications for the main assessment scales used in nursing and healthcare, which require calculations based on the sum of scores to identify risks or assist nurses in developing a care plan. Examples include: the Braden Scale, the Morse Scale, and the Fugulin Scale. In addition, the platform offers monitoring scales that support medical interventions, such as the Aldrete and Kroulik Scale, which are widely used by nursing staff and essential for guiding patient discharge from the Emergency Room (ER).

These scales are most often administered by the nursing staff and serve as a guide for determining care protocols by the entire multidisciplinary team—including nursing technicians, nurses, physicians, and physical therapists. In some of these scales, nurses play a more active role; in others, the tool serves only as a basis for monitoring to inform the physician’s decision. An example of this is the Aldrete Scale: although it is applied and monitored by the nursing staff in the SRPA, the final decision regarding the patient’s discharge or continued monitoring rests exclusively with the anesthesiologist.

The mathematical calculations provided by the calculators on this website are for informational and educational purposes only, based on recognized bibliographic references, which should be consulted to validate the content. Reviewing calculations for medications, insulin, and IV drips does not replace the manual application of traditional methods—such as the use of paper, pencil, and standardized mathematical rules. Under no circumstances should the calculators be considered the sole means of arriving at the final result. They serve as a comparative and educational aid but do not replace the professional’s critical thinking and technical responsibility. No automated calculation replaces—and will never replace—the analysis and judgment of a nursing professional.

If you have any questions about the formulation or results of the calculations, we recommend consulting your nursing instructor or the nurse in charge, who are qualified to provide clarification, answer questions, and guide you through the correct procedure for performing the calculations and obtaining the results.

Commitment to Digital Sustainability

We adopt practices focused on digital efficiency, including energy-efficient hosting, the use of WebP images, a modular structure, continuous performance optimization, and a lightweight cookie script. We regularly monitor the website using tools such as Website Carbon, EcoGrader, Google Lighthouse, Chrome DevTools, GTmetrix, and Pingdom.

Even while still under development, initial results indicate reduced resource consumption, continuous performance improvements, and a more efficient browsing experience. We are committed to constantly evolving by implementing annual digital efficiency goals and publishing periodic reports with auditable metrics.
