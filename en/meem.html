Mini Mental State Examination (MMSE)

Cognitive Screening

The Mini Mental State Examination (MMSE) is a widely used cognitive screening tool to identify cognitive deficits, including those associated with dementia. It is a quick and simple tool, composed of 11 questions that assess orientation, memory, attention, and language.
