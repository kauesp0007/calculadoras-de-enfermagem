Capurro Method

Gestational Age Estimation

The Capurro Method, or Capurro scale, is a clinical assessment method for the gestational age (GA) of a newborn. The somatoneurological version evaluates six characteristics. Select an option for each criterion to get the result.

Reference:

CAPURRO, H. et al. A simplified method for diagnosis of gestational age in the newborn infant. The Journal of Pediatrics, v. 93, n. 1, p. 120-122, 1978. DOI: 10.1016/s0022-3476(78)80153-x. Available at: https://doi.org/10.1016/S0022-3476(78)80153-X.
