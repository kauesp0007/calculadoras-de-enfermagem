Bishop Score

Assessment of Cervical Maturity

The Bishop Score assesses cervical maturity based on five factors to predict the likelihood of successful labor induction. Select an option for each criterion to calculate the total score.

Year Created: 1955

Authors: Dr. Edward H. Bishop

Area Employed: Maternity and Obstetric Centers

Criteria Evaluated: Dilation, Effacement, Consistency, Cervical Position, and Fetal Station

Who uses it?: Obstetricians, midwives, and obstetric nurses

References:
  • Nicholas, B. A.; Pittenger, P. K. A practical application of the Bishop score. National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), 2018.
  • Bishop EH. Pelvic scoring for elective induction. Obstet Gynecol. 1964 Aug;24:266-8.
