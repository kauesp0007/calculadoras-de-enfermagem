Bishop Score

Cervical Maturity Assessment

The Bishop Score assesses cervical maturity across five factors to predict the likelihood of successful labor induction. Select one option for each criterion to calculate the total score.

Quick Score Reference

  • Score 0-5: Unfavorable/Immature Cervix
  • Score 6-7: Gray Zone (Uncertain)
  • Score 8-13: Favorable/Mature Cervix

Historical Information

Year of Creation: 1955

Author: Dr. Edward H. Bishop

Clinical Setting: Maternity wards and obstetric centers

Evaluated Criteria: Dilation, Effacement, Consistency, Cervical Position, and Fetal Station

Used by: Obstetricians, gynecologists, and obstetric nurses
