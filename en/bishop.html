Bishop Score

Cervical Maturity Assessment

The Bishop Score assesses cervical maturity across five factors to predict the probability of a successful labor induction. Select an option for each criterion to calculate the total score.

References:
  • Bishop, E. H. (1964). Pelvic scoring for elective induction. Obstetrics & Gynecology, 24(2), 266-267.
  • NICHOLAS, B. A.; PITTENGER, P. K. A practical application of the Bishop score. National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), 2018.
