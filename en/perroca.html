Perroca Scale (PCS): Patient Classification System in Nursing

The Perroca Scale uses 13 indicators to classify the patient's degree of dependency and the complexity of required nursing care. Widely used to assess patient care complexity, especially in Spanish-speaking countries.

Perroca Patient Classification System (PCS)

Year created: 2000

Author: Marisa Aparecida de Lima Perroca.

Area of use: Hospitals.

Assessed criteria: Mental status, oxygenation, vital signs, nutrition, motility, locomotion, body care, eliminations, therapy, skin integrity, health education, and behavior.

Who uses it? Staff nurses and nurse managers.
