Perroca Scale (PCS)

Patient Classification System

The Perroca Scale uses 13 indicators to classify the patient's degree of dependency and the complexity of nursing care required. Select the score for each item.

Year Created: 2000

Author: Marisa Aparecida de Lima Perroca

Area of Use: Hospitals.

Criteria Assessed: Mental status, oxygenation, vital signs, nutrition and hydration, motility, locomotion, body care, elimination, therapeutics, skin/mucosal integrity, health education, behavior, and interpersonal relationships.

Used by?: Nurses.

References:
  • Perroca, M. G. (2011). Development and validity of a new patient classification instrument. Revista Latino-Americana de Enfermagem, 19(1), 58-66. Available at: https://www.scielo.br/j/rlae/a/yKk4cKk8Cqgq4gXFzYqj5Wc/
