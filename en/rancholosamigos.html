Rancho Los Amigos Scale (RLAS)

Assessment of Cognitive and Behavioral Patterns after Brain Injury

The Rancho Los Amigos Scale describes 8 levels of cognitive and behavioral recovery in patients with Acquired Brain Injury (ABI). Select the level that best applies to the patient to see the detailed description and care recommendation.
