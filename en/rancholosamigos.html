Rancho Los Amigos Scale (RLAS)

Assessment of Cognitive and Behavioral Patterns after Brain Injury

The Rancho Los Amigos Scale describes 8 levels of cognitive and behavioral recovery in patients with Acquired Brain Injury (ABI). Select the level that best applies to the patient to see the detailed description and care recommendation.

References:
  • DURAN, L. V. R. F. Escala de Rancho Los Amigos: o que é e como usar. **Scribd**, 2012. Available at: https://pt.scribd.com/document/541564694/Escala-Rancho-Los-Amigos. Accessed: Jul 20, 2024.
  • RANCHO LOS AMIGOS National Rehabilitation Center. **Rancho Los Amigos Levels of Cognitive Functioning Scale**. Available at: https://www.rancho.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/levels_of_cognitive_functioning.pdf. Accessed: Jul 20, 2024.
