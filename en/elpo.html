About the ELPO Scale

This calculator assists in assessing pressure injury risk in patients undergoing surgical procedures in the operating room.

  • Year Created: 2014
  • Author: Prof. Dr. Camila Mendonça de Moraes Lopes, School of Nursing at Ribeirão Preto (EERP), University of São Paulo (USP)
  • Clinical Setting: Operating room and post-anesthesia recovery unit
  • Primary Users: Operating room nurses

Evaluation Criteria

The ELPO Scale evaluates seven key criteria to determine pressure injury risk:

  1. Surgical position
  2. Surgery duration
  3. Type of anesthesia
  4. Support type
  5. Skin condition
  6. Comorbidities
  7. Nutritional status

Clinical Guidelines

Risk Interpretation

  • Score ≥ 20: High risk for pressure injury
  • Score < 20: Low risk for pressure injury

Assessment Timing

Perform assessment pre-operatively and during long surgical procedures to identify at-risk patients and implement appropriate preventive measures.
