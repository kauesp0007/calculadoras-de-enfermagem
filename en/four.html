FOUR Scale

Detailed Neurological Assessment with Emphasis on Brainstem

The FOUR Scale (Full Outline of UnResponsiveness) is a comprehensive neurological assessment tool, developed as an alternative or complement to the Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS). It evaluates four main components: Eye Response, Motor Response, Brainstem Reflexes, and Respiration.
