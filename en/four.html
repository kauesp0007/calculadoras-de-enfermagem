FOUR Score Calculator

Full Outline of UnResponsiveness (Coma Scale)

The FOUR Score (Full Outline of UnResponsiveness) is a clinical scale designed to assess consciousness in critically ill patients, especially those who are intubated. Unlike the GCS, it includes brainstem reflexes and respiration patterns, providing greater neurological detail.
