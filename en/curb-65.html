CURB-65 Score

Community-Acquired Pneumonia Severity Assessment

The CURB-65 Score is a tool for assessing the severity of pneumonia. For each present criterion, select "Yes" to add a point to the total score.

References:
  • LIM, W. S. et al. Defining community acquired pneumonia severity on presentation to hospital: an international derivation and validation study. **Thorax**, v. 58, n. 5, p. 377-382, 2003.
