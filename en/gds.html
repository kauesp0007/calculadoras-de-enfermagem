Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS)

Screening and Assessment of Depression in the Elderly

The Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS-15) is a screening tool to identify depressive symptoms in the elderly. It consists of 15 "yes/no" questions that assess mood, interest, and energy levels over the past few weeks.
