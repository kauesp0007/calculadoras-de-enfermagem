Berg Balance Scale (BBS)

Static and Dynamic Balance Assessment

The Berg Balance Scale (BBS) assesses an individual's balance through 14 routine tasks. Select the score for each task to obtain the total score and fall risk recommendation.

Quick Assessment Overview

  • Score Range: 0-56 points
  • Tasks Assessed: 14 balance-related activities
  • Assessment Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Professional Use: Physical therapists, occupational therapists

Year Created: 1992

Authors: Katherine O. Berg and collaborators

Areas Used: Physiotherapy, Gerontology and Rehabilitation

Criteria Assessed: 14 balance-related tasks

Who Uses It?: Physical therapists and occupational therapists
