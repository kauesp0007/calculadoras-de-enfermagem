NEWS Protocol

National Early Warning Score

The NEWS Protocol (National Early Warning Score) is a tool for early detection of clinical deterioration in hospitalized patients. The score is calculated from physiological variables, and if the result indicates risk, an action plan is triggered to ensure patient safety.
