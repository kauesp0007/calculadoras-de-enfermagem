Braden Scale

Pressure Injury Risk Assessment

The Braden Scale is a risk assessment tool for the development of pressure injuries (pressure ulcers). It evaluates six criteria: sensory perception, moisture, activity, mobility, nutrition, and friction/shear.

Scale Information

  • Year Created: 1987
  • Authors: Barbara Braden and Nancy Bergstrom
  • Clinical Area: Medical and surgical units (wards)
  • User: Nurses
