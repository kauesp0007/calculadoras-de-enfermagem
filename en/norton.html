Norton Scale

Pressure Injury Risk Assessment

The Norton Scale assesses the risk of developing pressure injuries in patients, considering five parameters: physical condition, mental state, activity, mobility, and incontinence.

Select the score for each criterion (each from 1 to 4 points). The lower the score, the higher the risk of pressure injury!
