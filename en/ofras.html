Obstetric Fall Risk Assessment (OFRAS) Calculator

Fall Risk Screening for Pregnant & Postpartum Patients

The OFRAS Scale (Obstetric Fall Risk Assessment System) is a specialized clinical tool to evaluate the risk of falls in obstetric patients. By assessing key factors such as history of falls, cardiac conditions, hemorrhage risk, anesthesia use, motor activity, and medication, nurses can implement targeted safety protocols to protect mothers and babies.
