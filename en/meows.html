MEOWS Protocol

Modified Early Obstetric Warning Score

The MEOWS Protocol (Modified Early Obstetric Warning Score) is a tool for monitoring and preventing clinical deterioration in obstetric patients. The score is calculated from physiological variables, and if the result indicates high risk, an action plan is triggered to ensure patient safety.
