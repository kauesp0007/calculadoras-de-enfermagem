Accessibility Policy

Commitment to Digital Inclusion and Usability for All Users

Our Commitment

Nursing Calculators is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, regardless of their abilities, providing an inclusive, usable, and barrier-free experience.

Accessibility Practices

  • Compliance with international accessibility standards (WCAG, WAI-ARIA)
  • Continuous improvement of usability and navigation
  • Accessible design for people with disabilities
  • Support for assistive technologies
  • Accessible content and calculators
  • Feedback channels for accessibility suggestions

Feedback and Contact

If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at ciadeenfermagem@gmail.com.
