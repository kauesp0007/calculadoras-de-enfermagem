Calculation Formulas

Time in HOURS:

  • Drops/min = Vol (ml) ÷ (Time (h) × 3)
  • Microdrops/min = Vol (ml) ÷ Time (h)

Time in MINUTES:

  • Drops/min = (Vol (ml) × 20) ÷ Time (min)
  • Microdrops/min = (Vol (ml) × 60) ÷ Time (min)

Reference:
INFUSION NURSES SOCIETY. Infusion Therapy Standards of Practice. 8th ed. Journal of Infusion Nursing, 2021. Available at: https://www.ins1.org/
