IV Drip Rate Calculation: Macrodrips and Microdrips

What is IV Drip Rate Calculation?

It is an essential mathematical formula in nursing to determine the exact speed of intravenous fluid administration, indicating the precise number of drops or microdrops per minute.

  • The Variables: Uses total Volume (V) in milliliters (mL) and Time (T) in hours or minutes.
  • Macrodrips (drops/min): Uses division factor 3 (for hours) or multiplier 20 (for minutes).
  • Microdrips (microdrops/min): Direct division of time (for hours) or uses multiplier 60 (for minutes).
