CAM-ICU Scale

Confusion Assessment Method for the ICU

The CAM-ICU Scale (Confusion Assessment Method for the Intensive Care Unit) is a validated instrument for assessing the presence of delirium in ICU patients, including those on mechanical ventilation. The assessment focuses on four criteria: acute onset of mental status change, inattention, altered level of consciousness, and disorganized thinking.

  • Year of Creation: 2001
  • Authors: Dr. E. Wesley Ely and collaborators (Ely et al.).
  • Area of Use: Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Criteria Assessed: Acute change in mental status, inattention, level of consciousness, and disorganized thinking.
  • Who Uses It?: Intensivist physicians and ICU nurses.
