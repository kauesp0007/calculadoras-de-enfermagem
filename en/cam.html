CAM-ICU Scale

Confusion Assessment Method for ICU

The CAM-ICU Scale (Confusion Assessment Method for the Intensive Care Unit) is a validated instrument to assess the presence of delirium in ICU patients, including those on mechanical ventilation. The assessment focuses on four criteria: acute mental status change, inattention, altered level of consciousness, and disorganized thinking.

Year Created: 2001

Authors: Dr. E. Wesley Ely and colleagues (Ely et al.).

Area Used: Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Criteria Assessed: Acute mental status change, inattention, level of consciousness, and disorganized thinking.

Who uses it?: Intensivist physicians and ICU nurses.
