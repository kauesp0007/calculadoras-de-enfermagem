CURB-65评分

社区获得性肺炎严重程度评估

CURB-65评分是评估肺炎严重程度的工具。对于每个存在的标准，选择"是"来在总分中加一分。

创建年份：2000年

作者：英国胸科学会 (BTS) 研究人员

应用领域：初级保健、急诊科和住院病房

评估标准：意识模糊、尿素、呼吸频率、血压和年龄

使用者：医生和护士

参考文献：
  • LIM, W. S. et al. Defining community acquired pneumonia severity on presentation to hospital: an international derivation and validation study. Thorax, v. 58, n. 5, p. 377-382, 2003. PMID: 12670831
  • Metlay, J. P. et al. Diagnosis and Treatment of Adults with Community-acquired Pneumonia. An Official Clinical Practice Guideline of the American Thoracic Society and Infectious Diseases Society of America. American Journal of Respiratory and Critical Care Medicine, v. 200, n. 7, p. e45-e67, 2019. PMID: 31573350
